Linguify

AI-powered multilingual document translation platform

The Challenge

Organizations working across languages often depend on manual translation processes that can take days and create bottlenecks in communication.

Impact

Reduced document translation turnaround from 3–4 days to under 2 minutes through an AI-powered workflow.

For a typical 12-page document (~6,000 words), the workflow demonstrated the potential to avoid approximately $1,500–$1,800 in freelance translation costs, based on market translation rates.
